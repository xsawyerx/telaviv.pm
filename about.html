About Us

About Perl Mongers

The Perl Mongers are a local group of (mostly, but not necessarily) Perl programmers who meet every month.
There is no registry of members, and anyone can come to any meeting. If you have the time, stop by!

Meetings

All meetings organized by TA.pm are completely 100% free-of-charge, and everyone is invited.
Anyone can give talks on almost any subject, while Perl-related subjects are obviously more recommended. :)
If you feel like giving a talk, contact the general Israel perl@perl.org.il mailing list, or contact Sawyer X directly.

The meetings are suitable for beginners, intermediates and advanced!
