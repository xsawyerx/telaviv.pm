Welcome to the Tel Aviv Perl Mongers group!

The group organizes for social events centered around Perl in the greater Tel Aviv, Israel area.
We have talks, lectures, and casual meetings.
Come meet fellow Perl (and non-Perl) geeks, share a snack, have some laughs!
(the meetings are suitable for beginners, intermediates and advanced)

Location

All meetings take place at Shenkar College, Anna Franck 12, Ramat Gan, room 323!
We get together at 6:30pm, and talks begin at 7:00pm.
